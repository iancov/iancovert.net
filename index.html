Twink '25 | Zine, 2024
A zine publishing of an essay I wrote about being a Twink. Includes research on the history, politics, and context of the term, as well as speculative plans for a project intended to synthesize and expand on these ideas. Printed in two layers on a Risograph, the pink spreads contain imagery sourced from James Bidgood's Pink Narcissus (1971).
tw(z)in(e)k | Zine Series, 2024
Three zines that explore the slang term Twink in the modern digital realm. Twink Defs compiles the top six definitions of the term on urbandictionary.com. Twink Tweets selects some of the most popular tweets containing the term. Twink Quiz asks the audience to discern who the real Twink is amongst photos of celebrities. Risograph printed on letter sized paper.
Various Poster Works | 2024
Posters developed for a variety of community events. Illustrations were hand-drawn; final comps were shared digitally and printed.
